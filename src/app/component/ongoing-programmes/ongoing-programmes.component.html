Our Work in Liberia

At ACCI, we recognize the potential of Liberia’s youth as catalysts for change. Through transformative initiatives like the Community Engagement Exchange (CEE) Workshop and the International Youth Day (IYD) Conference in 2024, we equipped young Liberians with the knowledge, tools, and platforms to lead sustainable development in their communities.

  • CEE Workshop: This hands-on workshop educated young people on how to access and maximize global opportunities and get rewarded and recognized for the work they do.
  • IYD Conference: In celebration of International Youth Day, ACCI convened youth leaders, educators, and policymakers to discuss youth-centered policies, entrepreneurship, climate action, and digital inclusion.

Our Vision for Liberia

We are committed to continuing our work in Liberia through youth empowerment, climate action, and education programs that align with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Liberia is full of changemakers—and we’re here to support, amplify, and equip them.

Our Work in Sierra Leone

ACCI is fostering climate leadership & innovation and youth development in Sierra Leone through strategic programs that equip young people with practical solutions to today’s pressing challenges.

  • Climate Workshop: Through this impactful 2-day workshop, participants learned how climate change affects their communities and what role they can play in driving local and global climate solutions.
  • Community Innovation Hub Project: We created safe, inspiring spaces for youth to learn, co-create, and collaborate. Focus areas include computer literacy, fashion design, cake making, arts & crafts, etc.

Our Vision for Sierra Leone

We believe in the creativity, resilience, and leadership of Sierra Leonean youth. Our work is focused on building their capacity to lead climate resilience efforts, drive innovation, and co-create thriving, inclusive communities.
