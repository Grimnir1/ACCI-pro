Community Innovation Hubs for Youth Empowerment and Entrepreneurship in West Africa

Community Innovation Hubs in Ghana, Liberia, Sierra Leone, and Nigeria empower young people in underserved areas by offering informal education in digital literacy, entrepreneurship, and vocational skills. These hubs provide free internet access, bridging the gap between education and employment while equipping youth with the tools to thrive in today's fast-evolving world.

Beyond skill-building, the hubs encourage innovation and entrepreneurship, helping youth turn ideas into businesses. They foster a collaborative environment for mentorship and knowledge-sharing, playing a key role in driving economic growth and improving opportunities for marginalized youth.
